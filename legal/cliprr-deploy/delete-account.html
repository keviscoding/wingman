Delete Your Muzo Account

Last updated April 27, 2026

You can permanently delete your Muzo account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this — pick whichever is easier for you.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Muzo app on your phone.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the home screen).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers immediately.

Option 2 — Email request

If you can no longer access the app or prefer to delete by email, send a request to contact@cliprr.io from the email address associated with your Muzo account.

Include the subject line Delete my Muzo account. We will confirm receipt within 1 business day and complete the deletion within 7 business days.

What gets deleted

Once you confirm deletion (in-app or by email), the following data is permanently removed:

  1. Your account record (email, display name, password hash).
  2. All chats you've created and the reply suggestions generated for them.
  3. All generation history and usage records associated with your account.
  4. Your push notification token (you'll stop receiving notifications immediately).
  5. Any active or queued background jobs (e.g. in-progress generations).

What is retained

For tax and accounting purposes, payment records held by Apple or Google (the parties that processed any subscription payments) may be retained by them according to their own retention policies. Muzo does not store payment information directly.

Anonymized, aggregated logs (which contain no personally identifying information and cannot be linked to your account once deleted) may be retained for up to 90 days for security and abuse-prevention purposes.

Subscription cancellation

Important: Deleting your Muzo account does not automatically cancel an active App Store or Google Play subscription. To cancel a subscription, you must do so through your App Store / Play Store subscription settings before deleting your account.

Cancellation links:

  1. iOS: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions
  2. Android: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
